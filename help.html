Professional Summary

  • Keep it concise (2-3 sentences)
  • Highlight your key strengths and career goals
  • Use action words and quantify achievements when possible

Work Experience

  • List experiences in reverse chronological order (most recent first)
  • Use bullet points for job descriptions
  • Focus on achievements, not just responsibilities
  • Include specific numbers and results when possible

Skills Section

  • List relevant technical and soft skills
  • Separate with commas for easy reading
  • Include both hard skills (software, languages) and soft skills (leadership, communication)

üîß Troubleshooting

PDF Not Downloading?

  • Make sure you've entered at least your full name
  • Check if your browser is blocking pop-ups
  • Try using a different browser (Chrome, Firefox, Safari)
  • Disable ad blockers temporarily

Formatting Issues?

  • Keep text concise to avoid overflow
  • Use line breaks in text areas for better formatting
  • Try switching templates if content doesn't fit well

Data Not Saving?

  • Make sure your browser allows local storage
  • Clear browser cache and try again
  • Don't use private/incognito mode

üéØ Best Practices

Resume Length

  • Entry Level: 1 page maximum
  • Experienced (5+ years): 1-2 pages
  • Senior/Executive: 2-3 pages maximum

ATS-Friendly Tips

  • Use standard section headings (Work Experience, Education, Skills)
  • Include relevant keywords from job descriptions
  • Use simple, clean formatting
  • Save as PDF to preserve formatting

Common Mistakes to Avoid

  • Typos and grammatical errors
  • Using unprofessional email addresses
  • Including irrelevant personal information
  • Making the resume too long or too short
  • Using fancy fonts or excessive colors

‚ùì Frequently Asked Questions

How do I access FastCV-LTS?

FastCV-LTS is a premium resume builder. Access is provided after purchase through your unique link.

Do you store my personal information?

No, we don't store your data on our servers. Everything is saved locally in your browser.

Can I edit my resume after downloading?

Yes! You can upload your PDF resume file to edit it. FastCV-LTS can extract text from PDF files and populate the form fields automatically. You can also upload the .json data file that's saved with your PDF for perfect restoration. Note: After refreshing the page, your resume will be deleted as we don't store any information for privacy reasons.

How do I upload a resume to edit?

Use the "Upload Existing Resume to Edit" section at the top of the builder page. You can upload PDF files (text will be extracted automatically), JSON data files (for perfect restoration), or TXT files. The resume will open in a new tab for editing. The upload will not save after refreshing for privacy protection.

What browsers are supported?

FastCV-LTS works best on modern browsers: Chrome, Firefox, Safari, and Edge.

Can I use this on mobile?

Yes! Our builder is mobile-responsive, though we recommend using a desktop for the best experience.
